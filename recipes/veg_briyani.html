Veg-Biryani Recipe

Description

Veg Biryani is a fragrant and spiced rice dish made with mixed vegetables, basmati rice, and various aromatic spices. It's a popular Indian dish known for its savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, bell pepper, cauliflower)
  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder, bay leaves, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Fresh mint and cilantro
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Salt

Recipe Steps

  1. Rinse and soak basmati rice.
  2. Fry sliced onions until golden, set aside.
  3. In the same oil, saut√© ginger-garlic paste, then add tomatoes, and cook until mushy.
  4. Add mixed vegetables, yogurt, and all the spices. Cook until the veggies are slightly tender.
  5. Layer the partially cooked veggies and rice in a pot. Top with fried onions, mint, cilantro, and saffron-infused milk.
  6. Cover and cook on a low flame until the rice is done.
