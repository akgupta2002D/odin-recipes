Kaju-Katli Recipe

Description

Kaju Katli is a popular Indian sweet made with cashew nuts and sugar.

It's known for its smooth texture and diamond shape.

Ingredients

  • Cashew nuts
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee
  • Silver vark (edible silver leaf, optional)

Recipe Steps

  1. Powder the cashews finely.
  2. Make a sugar syrup until it reaches one-string consistency.
  3. Add the cashew powder to the syrup, mix well.
  4. When the mixture thickens, remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. Knead into a smooth dough and roll it out on a greased surface.
  6. Apply silver leaf if using, and cut into diamond shapes.
